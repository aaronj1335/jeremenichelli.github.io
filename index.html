Experience

After some time working on small sites for companies that were settled on my home town while studying, I decided to make some change and decided to dropped it since I felt I wasn't really learning and enjoying it as before.

But that doesn't mean I stopped studying, from early time in the morning and til evening came I read books and articles about web development trying to focus on becoming the best possible Front End developer I could be.

That same year, I was hired by Globant and started working for big clients like Microsoft, StateFarm and New York Stock Exchange between others building awesome responsive web sites.

Now, I'm currently working in OrderGroove powering the subscription revolution using JavaScript.
